Real estate broker
==================

The real estate broker product turns plone into a real estate broker
website. Show your commercial and residential real estate with the two real
estate content types. Allow visitors to search the database with the provided
forms. Email updates for registered visitors. Google map support. Easy
addition of images.

Commercial and residential real estate content types
----------------------------------------------------
